The problem with business planning today

Business plans fail — not because of bad ideas, but because of strategic incoherence. The model your investor saw is never the model your team executes.

  • Static documents become obsolete the moment they're saved
  • No traceability between problems, features, and metrics
  • No validation that your value proposition actually solves the stated problem

"Companies don't die from lack of ideas. They die from strategic incoherence."

FORMAT is different

FORMAT is a plain-text, structured Markdown specification that lives in your Git repository alongside your code. One file. Your entire business model.

Version-controlled living models

Your business strategy evolves as pull requests. Every change is tracked, diffable, and reversible. Strategic PRs are a real thing now.

Automated strategic coherence

An AI engine validates the consistency between your problems, value propositions, features, and metrics — and delivers a coherence score you can act on.

Complete strategic traceability

Every dimension of your business (team, finance, roadmap, customer problems) is connected in a multidimensional relational graph — no more silos.

How it works

  1. Connect your local repository to the FORMAT editor
  2. Declare your stakeholders, problems, and value propositions in plain Markdown
  3. Map bidirectional relationships in matrix tables
  4. Validate — AI and logic engine deliver a coherence report with scores
  5. Save directly to your repo and iterate with your team
