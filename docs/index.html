One file. Infinite possibilities.

_FORMAT keeps your entire business model in a single Markdown file that humans read, AI understands, and Git versions.

📄

Plain Markdown

Author in any text editor. Review in GitHub. The format is designed for both human readability and machine processing.

🔗

Built-in Relations

Define matrix tables that map how stakeholders, problems, value propositions, and metrics interconnect in your business.

🤖

AI-Native

Drop your model into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. The AI instantly understands your full business context without extra prompting.

📊

Visual Graph View

Open your model in the FORMAT app to see an interactive graph of all concepts and relationships — Sankey or force-directed layouts.

📦

Version-Controlled

Commit your model to Git. Track changes, review diffs, collaborate with your team — just like code.

🛠️

Open Spec

Fully open specification. Build your own tools, integrations, and generators. No vendor lock-in.

How it works

1

Connect

Create a _FORMAT file from a template or clone an existing model to get started.

2

Model

Declare your stakeholders, problems, value propositions, market segments, and more in structured Markdown blocks.

3

Map

Define relationships between concepts using simple matrix tables. The app visualizes these connections automatically.

4

Validate

The app scores the coherence of your model, highlighting gaps and suggesting improvements.

5

Use

Drop your file into any AI model or share it with stakeholders. Your entire business fits in one portable file.
